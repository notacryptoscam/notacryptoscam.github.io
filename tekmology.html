NotACryptoScam.io


Tekmology

NotACryptoScam($NACS) is the first attempt at a blockchain-less cryptocurrency token.


Blockchain Alternative

There are many problems with the popular blockchains in crypto as of 2021. The Bitcoin network uses as much energy as Sweden(0.55% of global electricity production). The Ethereum network uses as much energy as a large town(2.62 megawatts). These numbers are unsustainable, and increase Global Warming.

$NACS attempts to fix these issues by not having a blockchain at all. As opposed to Proof of Work(PoW) or Proof of Stake(PoS), $NACS uses a Proof of Ownership system. Instead of mining the blockchain, or running nodes(both of which waste a lot of power), $NACS uses a unified transaction ledger system. This allows us to be one of the greenest tokens of all time.

$NACS runs on a Structured Query Language database, which offers all the advantages of a blockchain, without all of its disadvantages such as high power usage.

All of the clients connect to the central Structured Query Language database and perform operations on it indirectly through our transaction system.
